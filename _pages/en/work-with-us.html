Interested in Joining CDS?

At the Canadian Digital Service (CDS), we're passionate about making it easier to access and use government services. We are driven by empathy in our approach to discovery and design, and we use proven technologies to get the job done.

The Job Details

We're hiring in five streams for our delivery and engagement teams, with positions available at a variety of levels and classifications, according to the Government of Canada standards. Our positions will be flexible, and contracts are different lengths. We're currently hiring in Ottawa, but eventually we plan to have teams working across the country.

What we're looking for

A passion for providing better services for Canadians.
Resiliency in the face of adversity. This job may just be the hardest you will have.
A focus on delivery, with the success of your team as your first priority.
The ability to create structure out of ambiguity, and to forge a path in new environments.
In the face of challenges, the ability to embrace new ideas.
Teammates who are diverse in gender, indigenous heritage, race, (dis)ability, age, and language.
